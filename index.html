Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix bread with a little water to form a dough.
  2. Shape the dough into small balls.
  3. Fry the balls in oil until golden brown.
  4. Soak the fried balls in sugar syrup.

Kaju Barfi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cashew nuts (kaju)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water

Recipe

  1. Grind nuts to powder.
  2. Add sugar and water, heat until sugar dissolves.
  3. Cook the mixture on low heat and stirring.
  4. When it thickens grease it with ghee.
  5. Spread it evenly using spatula on a plate.
  6. Let it cool, then cut in diamond-shaped pieces.
